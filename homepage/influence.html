Influence By Caldini

Ever wonder why some people have all the influence while some people, with nothing? Human beings are social creatures, turns out there are psychological triggers that every human being will be affected by. Influence explores this..

Key Takeaways: Influence By Cialdini
  • People have a natural inclination to follow authority even if they are wrong.
  • Social proof, shows that if things are in demand, people naturally follow what other people follow, even if they are wrong.
  • Everything is relative, comparing something with something gives us projected value. Even though the inherent value has never changed.
  • People are more likely to continue a diet if they start it from a small commitment
  • Know that you are easily manipulated, knowledge is power to counteract this.
