The Shallows

Our brains have been fried. Shallows explore how the internet has changed the way we think, interact and remember with information. Never before a book that's so important in our digital age.

Key Takeaways: The Shallows
  • Nicholas Carr is a genius.
  • Don't believe what you see in social media, it's only an image.
  • We need to learn how to focus as our focus is completely being screwed up by social media.
  • Spend more time in nature and with your loved ones.
  • Don't attach self esteem to social media and internet.
